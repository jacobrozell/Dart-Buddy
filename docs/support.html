Common questions

How do I delete all my data?
Open Settings → Reset All Local Data → confirm. This permanently removes players, match history, and settings on that device.

Does the app require an account?
No. Dart Buddy works offline with data stored on your device.

Does the app show ads?
No. Dart Buddy is free and ad-free.

Where is my data stored?
On your iPhone or iPad. See our Privacy Policy for details on optional diagnostics in App Store builds.
