# Dart Buddy — Launch Plan & Content Calendar

> A lean, quality-first launch plan sized for a solo developer. Phases are sequential gates, not
> fixed dates — advance only when the previous phase's exit criteria are met.

---

## Phase 0 — Foundations (before any promotion)

**Goal:** the listing converts and the app is stable before anyone is pointed at it.

- [ ] Finalize store copy from `APP_STORE_LISTING.md` in App Store Connect.
- [ ] Upload iPhone + iPad screenshots (dark hero shots first), with captions.
- [ ] Support + Privacy pages live (`docs/support.html`, `docs/privacy.html`).
- [ ] App Privacy labels verified against actual behavior.
- [ ] Crash-free build validated on a physical device via TestFlight.
- [ ] In-app ratings prompt wired to a delight moment (e.g., after a won match), throttled.
- [ ] Landing page (`docs/`) shows hero + 3 benefits + App Store badge.

**Exit criteria:** stable RC build, complete + truthful listing, working support/privacy URLs.

---

## Phase 1 — TestFlight beta

**Goal:** real darts players use it, surface top issues, gather testimonials.

- Recruit 15–50 testers from r/Darts, league groups, Discord, and personal network.
- Give testers a one-line ask: *"Score a few real games and tell me the single most annoying
  thing."*
- Triage feedback; fix the top friction points and any crashes.
- Collect: quotes for social proof, screenshots of real use, feature requests for 1.x.

**Exit criteria:** no known crashers, top-3 friction items resolved, ≥1 round of beta feedback
incorporated.

---

## Phase 2 — Soft launch

**Goal:** go live quietly and confirm the listing converts before amplifying.

- Submit for review; release to the App Store.
- Privately invite the beta cohort to download the public version and leave an honest rating.
- Watch: product-page conversion, activation (% completing ≥1 match), crash-free rate.
- Fix anything embarrassing fast; don't amplify until conversion looks healthy.

**Exit criteria:** listing live, first ratings in, conversion + stability look acceptable.

---

## Phase 3 — Announcement

**Goal:** drive the first real wave of high-intent installs.

- **Community posts** (honest, value-first, per each community's rules):
  - r/Darts: "I built a free, no-ads darts scoreboard for X01 & Cricket — feedback welcome."
  - League Facebook groups / Discords: short, helpful, with a screenshot.
  - Relevant darts forums.
- **Owned:** publish a short landing/blog post; share on personal social.
- **Social video:** post the "10-second leg, no ads" clip to Reels/Shorts/TikTok.
- **Ask for reviews** from happy users explicitly (honest reviews only).

**Exit criteria:** announcement live across chosen communities; review volume rising.

---

## Phase 4 — Sustain & iterate (first 90 days)

**Goal:** compound ASO and trust; ship a responsive patch cycle.

- **Weekly:** respond to every App Store review; one helpful community post/comment.
- **Bi-weekly:** review ASO data — adjust subtitle/keywords/screenshot order.
- **As needed:** ship 1.0.1+ patches; mention fixes in "What's New" and reply to reviewers who
  reported them (shows responsiveness).
- **~Day 30:** replace the directional KPI targets in `MARKETING_STRATEGY.md` §10 with real
  baselines; decide whether to test Apple Search Ads.
- **~Day 60–90:** ship the first retention feature from the roadmap (e.g., play reminders or
  Game Center achievements) and announce it.

## Content ideas bank

Reusable angles — match to channel and cadence as capacity allows.

**Short video (Reels / Shorts / TikTok):**
- 10-second leg scored start-to-finish → "No ads. No login." punchline.
- Split-screen: cluttered ad-app vs. Dart Buddy's clean board.
- "Can you find the checkout?" → reveal the in-app checkout suggester.
- Bot challenge: play the Pro bot, react to the finish.

**Static / carousel:**
- "3 reasons darts players are switching" (no ads · no account · real stats).
- Before/after: a messy paper scorecard vs. the app's history view.
- Annotated stats screen: "what your 3-dart average actually means."

**Written (forums / blog / Reddit):**
- "How to read your darts stats: averages, MPR, and checkout %."
- "X01 checkouts cheat sheet" (evergreen, links the app naturally).
- Honest "why I built this" dev post — builds trust in communities.

**Evergreen utility (drives ASO long-tail + backlinks):**
- Checkout chart, Cricket rules explainer, X01 vs. Cricket beginner's guide on the `docs/` site.

## Definition of a successful launch (first 90 days)

- Ranking on page 1 for core niche terms (e.g., "X01", "cricket darts scorer").
- 4.5★+ average with a steadily growing review count.
- ≥60% of installs complete at least one match (activation).
- Crash-free rate ≥ 99.5%.
- A real baseline for D1/D7/D30 retention to optimize against.
- A small but genuine base of returning players and a feedback channel feeding the 1.x roadmap.
