# Dart Buddy — Marketing

Marketing strategy and App Store materials for **Dart Buddy**, a free, no-ads, local-first
darts scorekeeper for X01 and Cricket (iPhone + iPad, iOS 17+).

This folder is the source of truth for go-to-market planning and store copy. It complements,
and does not replace, the engineering-facing [`specs/AppStoreConnectSpec.md`](../specs/AppStoreConnectSpec.md)
(which governs identifiers, asset sizes, and release process).

## Contents

| Document | What it's for |
|----------|---------------|
| [`MARKETING_STRATEGY.md`](MARKETING_STRATEGY.md) | Positioning, audience, messaging, channels, ASO, growth, and KPIs |
| [`APP_STORE_LISTING.md`](APP_STORE_LISTING.md) | Ready-to-paste store copy: name, subtitle, keywords, description, promo text, "What's New", and screenshot captions |
| [`CONTENT_AND_LAUNCH_CALENDAR.md`](CONTENT_AND_LAUNCH_CALENDAR.md) | Pre-launch → launch → post-launch timeline, content ideas, and channel cadence |

## App at a glance

- **Name:** Dart Buddy
- **Category:** Sports · **Price:** Free · **Ads:** None · **IAP:** None (1.0)
- **Platforms:** iPhone + iPad, iOS 17+
- **Modes:** X01 (301/501, single/double-out) and Cricket (Normal / Cut Throat, points on/off)
- **Differentiators:** No ads, no account required, local-first, fast input, calibrated bot opponents, stats & history, accessible (WCAG 2.1 AA target), localized (en/de/es/nl)
- **App Store:** https://apps.apple.com/app/id6775713346

## How to use this

1. Read `MARKETING_STRATEGY.md` to align on positioning and audience.
2. Copy final listing text from `APP_STORE_LISTING.md` into App Store Connect.
3. Execute against `CONTENT_AND_LAUNCH_CALENDAR.md`.

> Source assets (screenshots, framed device shots, app icon) live in
> [`../marketing-screenshots/`](../marketing-screenshots/) and `Resources/Media.xcassets`.
> Keep all claims truthful to shipped behavior — engineering signs off on metadata accuracy.
